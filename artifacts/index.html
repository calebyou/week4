Don't make connecting awkward

No more fumbling for business cards or searching for lost contacts after an event. Jive makes it easy to exchange info and keep the conversation going straight from your inbox.

Here's how it works

Scan the QR code

When it's time to swap info, have your new contact scan your QR code with their phone camera.

Send a message

Your QR code is linked to a custom message you've created. So all they have to do is hit Send.

Follow-up from your inbox

At the end of the event, you can quickly scan your inbox and reply to everyone you met.
